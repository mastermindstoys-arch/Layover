Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and support.

01 What services does Layover Solutions offer?

We provide comprehensive digital solutions including website development, website enhancement, e-commerce solutions, social commerce integration, AI solutions, automation enhancement, payment gateways, and ongoing service support.

02 How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Basic websites take 2-4 weeks, medium-scale projects take 4-8 weeks, and enterprise solutions can take 8-16 weeks or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

03 Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages. Please reach us for more details.

04 What technologies do you work with?

We work with modern technologies including React, Angular, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, PHP and various AI/ML frameworks. We choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements.

05 Can you work with existing websites or applications?

Absolutely! Our website enhancement service specializes in upgrading and optimizing existing websites. We can improve performance, add new features, enhance UX/UI, and migrate to modern technologies while preserving your current data and functionality.

06 What is your development process?

Our process includes: 1) Requirement Discussion - understanding your needs, 2) Development & Testing - building robust solutions with quality assurance, and 3) Demo & Launch - presenting the solution and providing ongoing support.
